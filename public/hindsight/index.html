AnyQuestion
acquired by Whoop!
We celebrated the acquisition of AnyQuestion, Inc. by Whoop, Inc. AnyQuestion is a premium social video app that lets members ask questions and get answers from world-class experts. The acquisition ties in with the launch of Whoop Coach, an AI-powered coaching feature.

ILABs played a pivotal role in this success, having designed and developed the technology that fueled AnyQuestions's remarkable journey to acquisition. We also an early investor in AnyQuestion.

FitOn keeps
expanding it's reach
We have worked with FitOn from day 1, starting with the first line of code across every aspect of the product - Design, iOS / Android, Web app, Apple and Google TV apps, backend and more. We have obsessed over every detail with the FitOn team and the results speak for themselves. FitOn has been recognized as the "App of the Day'', secured the top spot as the #1 Health & Fitness app, and successfully acquired & integrated fitness benefit provider Peerfit to enter the healthcare space. Just last month, out of 2 million apps, Apple named FitOn as one of their 2023 App Store Awards Finalists. The FitOn team is firing on all cylinders, and we are excited to be part of their journey.
